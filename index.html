Our Story

Welcome to Namo Buddha (नमो बुद्धा), a traditional rural farmhouse project located in the heart of the village. Currently under construction, our vision is to create a serene retreat where farming, nature, and simple living come together. Our fields are home to fresh crops and beautiful landscapes, offering visitors a peaceful escape from city life.
